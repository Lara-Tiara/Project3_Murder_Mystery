After a long time without finding the murderer, the captain of the guards was furious and locked up all three of you. As the case was exposed, external pressures increased, and the three of you were collectively pushed forward to take on all the blame, being labeled as part of a criminal gang. You all knew well that they just needed scapegoats; it didn't really matter who was actually guilty. Who killed, you, me, or her? Who cares? All of this will become a part of history with the sound of three gunshots at the execution site, and the unknown stories will inevitably be lost to the wind...
